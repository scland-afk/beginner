Who We Are

Edmonton's Trusted Landscaping Professionals

Serene Construction Landscaping is a trusted landscaping company serving Edmonton and nearby communities including St. Albert, Spruce Grove, and Sherwood Park. We work with homeowners and property managers who want clean, functional outdoor spaces built with care and attention to detail.

We explore your property to get an idea of what can be transformed and present our landscaping designs for your approval. We discuss the planting, sodding, and other landscaping design requirements to make the beauty happen.

There's hidden beauty on your property that you may not see. But when you meet with our experts, the opportunity to visualize that hidden beauty is made clear. Our commitment to design, durability, and detail sets us apart from other landscaping companies in Edmonton.
