Our Services
What We Offer

Complete Landscaping Services

From landscape design and construction to ongoing maintenance, we provide comprehensive services for residential and commercial properties throughout Edmonton and surrounding areas.

  • Landscape Design & Construction

    Complete landscape design and construction services from initial planning to installation. Patios, walkways, turf, grading, and outdoor living areas built with proper preparation and long-term durability in mind.

  • Lawn Maintenance

    A healthy lawn takes more than occasional mowing. Our lawn care services are built around soil health, proper timing, and consistent maintenance to keep your lawn green and resilient through the growing season.

  • Snow Removal

    Edmonton winters require dependable snow clearing. We provide residential and commercial snow removal services to keep your property safe and accessible throughout the winter months.

  • Patios & Retaining Walls

    From simple patios to multi-level outdoor living spaces with retaining walls, we design and build hardscape features that add beauty and function to your property.

  • Fences & Gates

    A well-built fence adds privacy, structure, and value to your property. We build wood and composite fences with proper footings, solid framing, and clean finishes built to last.

  • Final Grading

    Proper grading is essential for drainage and the longevity of your landscape. We ensure your property has the correct slope to direct water away from structures and prevent pooling.

  • Tree Services

    From tree planting to trimming and removal, we provide complete tree care services. Our team ensures proper techniques for healthy trees and safe removal when needed.

  • Commercial Services

    We serve commercial properties with landscaping, lawn care, and snow removal. Keep your business looking professional year-round with our reliable commercial services.
